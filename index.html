Got questions? We’ve got answers

Bonanza Tours specializes in premium houseboat cruises, backwater explorations, and tailormade travel experiences across Kerala. We provide both day and overnight packages, along with curated sightseeing tours, local experiences, and group bookings for families, couples, and corporate teams.

Our cruise durations range from short half day getaways to multi day luxury experiences. You can choose the timing that best fits your schedule, whether you’re seeking a peaceful afternoon on the water or a fullfledged backwater retreat.

All our houseboats are equipped with modern comforts such as airconditioned bedrooms, ensuite bathrooms, spacious dining areas, and lounges with panoramic views. Guests can also enjoy freshly prepared Kerala cuisine and attentive onboard service throughout the trip.

Our pricing is transparent and based on the package type, duration, and level of amenities chosen. We provide detailed cost breakdowns with no hidden fees, ensuring that you know exactly what’s included before you confirm your booking.

Yes, all our cruises include delicious, freshly prepared meals made by our inhouse chefs. We serve authentic Kerala cuisine, with vegetarian and nonvegetarian options available. Special dietary preferences can be accommodated upon request.

Definitely. We offer a range of boats designed to suit families, couples, and large groups. Whether you’re planning a romantic getaway, a family vacation, or a corporate outing, we ensure a comfortable and memorable experience for everyone.

Your safety is our top priority. All our boats are government approved, regularly inspected, and operated by trained professionals. Life jackets, firstaid kits, and emergency protocols are always in place to ensure a safe and pleasant journey.
