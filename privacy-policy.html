Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 30, 2025

At Bonanza Tours, your privacy and trust are important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you interact with our website, make bookings, or use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

Personal Information: Name, phone number, email address, and payment details provided during booking.

Usage Data: Information about how you use our website, such as IP address, browser type, and visited pages.

Optional Details: Special requests, feedback, or preferences you share with us before or during your trip.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information only for legitimate business purposes, including:

Processing and confirming your bookings.

Providing personalized service and trip updates.

Managing payments, billing, and refunds.

Sending booking confirmations, reminders, or promotional offers (only with your consent).

Improving our website, packages, and guest experience.

3. Data Protection & Security

We take reasonable administrative and technical measures to safeguard your information from unauthorized access, misuse, or loss.

All digital transactions are processed through secure, encrypted channels.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal data.

We may share limited information only with:

Authorized service providers (e.g., payment processors or booking platforms).

Legal authorities if required by law or court order.

5. Cookies & Website Tracking

Our website may use cookies to improve functionality and analyze website performance.

You can adjust your browser settings to refuse cookies if you prefer.

6. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to other sites (such as payment gateways or partner agencies).

We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those third-party sites.

7. Data Retention

We retain booking and payment information only as long as necessary for legal and accounting purposes or to provide continued service support.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

Access, correct, or delete your personal data.

Withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time.

To make such requests, please contact us directly.

9. Updates to This Policy

Bonanza Tours reserves the right to modify this Privacy Policy when necessary. Updates will be posted on our website with a new effective date.
